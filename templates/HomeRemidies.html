AI-based awareness & supportive guidance

⚠ Medical Disclaimer:
This information is for awareness only. Cancerous skin diseases require immediate dermatologist consultation.
Pre-Cancerous

Actinic Keratoses (AKIEC)

  • Avoid direct sunlight
  • Use SPF 50+ sunscreen daily
  • Wear protective clothing
  • Consult dermatologist
Cancerous

Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC)

  • ❌ No home remedies
  • Immediate medical treatment required
  • Early diagnosis prevents spread
Benign

Benign Keratosis (BKL)

  • Maintain proper skin hygiene
  • Use mild moisturizer
  • Avoid scratching lesions
Benign

Dermatofibroma (DF)

  • Usually harmless
  • Avoid repeated skin injury
  • Consult doctor if painful
Very Dangerous

Melanoma (MEL)

  • ❌ NO home remedies
  • Immediate dermatologist consultation
  • Early detection saves life
Common Mole

Melanocytic Nevus (NV)

  • Monitor size and color
  • Use sunscreen regularly
  • Consult if changes occur
Benign

Vascular Lesion (VASC)

  • Usually harmless
  • Avoid skin injury
  • Consult doctor for cosmetic concern
